Santa Rosa, California

A Whole New World

Santa Rosa was interesting. I adapted to the change rather quickly. I still remember the friends I made in the fourth grade; Justin and Ryan. They were some of my closest friends. I eventually moved over to the next town of Windsor. Things in Windsor didn't go so well for me. But times and seasons as such as those in Windsor have helped mold me, and teach me how to overcome adversity.

My first job was in Windsor. Windsor and Santa Rosa is where I grew up. But this is also where I encountered a lot of hardship.

Moving Day

I wasn't anticipating much change. In fact, I was fearful, as a child of moving away from what I knew. But there was also excitement.

Jobs

I had many fast-food jobs in Santa Rosa. These jobs helped me learn customer service. I have always been a people-person. I like to talk to people, and these jobs just fueled that interest.

Fun-time

This is where I fell in love with basketball. I played in a lot of city leagues; I even played in middle school. One day while I was playing basketball at my friend's house in the rain, I badly broke my leg; this was one of the worst accidents I have ever experienced.
