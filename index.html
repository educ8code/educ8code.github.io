Welcome to My Webpage

Thank you for visiting my project page. I am creating this page as a part of my CIT-82 class at Fresno City College, and as a way of beginning to build my portfolio for what I hope turns into a promising future of Web Development!
Please enjoy and look forward to the feedback box.

About Me:

I am a husband, and a father of three beautiful children. My full-time job is to care for them. I am also a full-time student. I am pursuing a career in Web Development with the end-goal of being self employed. I have been using computer for over twenty-five years. I remember when America Online first came out; in fact, I can still hear the dial-up tone in my head. I am passionate of family, learning, and life. I love to read, teach, and share a common meal with loved ones. Thank you for taking the time to learn a little bit about me. Enjoy!

Why Are You Taking CIT-82?

I am taking this course in hopes of learning how to develope Websites, with the end-goal of working from home while I home-school my children. I either want to work remotely or own my own home-based business.

Are You Enjoying Yourself?

Very much so! I have not learned so much in four-weeks in any other course I have taken in college. The lesson plan is fabulous, and I believe I am on a fast-track to my desired goal.
