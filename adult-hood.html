Fresno, California

I'm Sweating Just Thinking About It

I got married in 2011, and I moved to Fresno to be with my lovely bride. She was well established here, so we decided to stay here. Within months of moving here, I wanted out; it is so hot here in the Central Valley, but over the years I have gotten used to it. I love living here today, and enjoy very much the amenities and food establishments that this area has to offer. There's a lot of food choices here in the Valley. One of my favorite things to do every October is the Big Fresno Fair.

When I first moved to Fresno, I wasn't certain about what I wanted to do. I have a passion for cooking, and I put a lot of thought into attending culinary school, but the stark reality of me working 12-15 hours a day scared me away. My wife and I wanted a family, so such a rigorous schedule wouldn't cut it.
It wasn't long after the chapel bells that my wife and I had our first child. We had a son. We had a beautiful baby boy, who is now moving on into kindergarten; time surely does fly.
Please be sure to stop by and say hello to my family.
