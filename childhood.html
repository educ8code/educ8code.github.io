Growing Up In Santa Cruz

From what I can remember...

I remember a little, but from what I do remember of Santa Cruz is all of the fun. I was born in Watsonville, a community of Santa Cruz County. Much of my family lived in Santa Cruz, and we regularly got together. My grandparents home was the epicenter of both food and fun.

I remember all of the Christmases spent with my grandparents, aunts and uncles, counsins, and siblings; it is interesting how as we grow older, those relationships tend to fade. I used to go to the community pool with my grandmother quite often, and my favorite part about those excursions, was the snack bar. We used to attend the Fourth of July Parade, which was held annually in Aptos, CA.

There were two things which I loved most about Santa Cruz: 1) The Santa Cruz Beach Boardwalk and 2) Cruz Kidz. Cruz Kidz was a summer camp I would attend each year. Sadly I don't think it is around anymore. The videos below depict much of Santa Cruz. The scenery is great, and they bring back many fond memories. Please take some time to view them. They have been provided by YouTube users - unknown.
