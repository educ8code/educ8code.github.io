My Marriage

I'm Her's and She's Mine

It was early 2011, and we made the decision of a lifetime; a decision that would alter our lives forever. It was the right decision. A decision I will never regret. I am so thankful to my wife, and her willingness to put up with me, and give herself to me. It wasn't long after we said our I DOs, that we ventured together to Disney World for our honeymoon. Disney World was so fun. My wife made it so special. We ordered a custom cake to celebrate our occassion.
We have been married now for 7 years, and each year has been special. We celebrate our wedding anniversary each year with a dish from Olive Garden and one of our favorite shows or movies. Our anniversary of 2013 was especially special because we were in the hospital set to deliver our son. We will never forget such moments.
