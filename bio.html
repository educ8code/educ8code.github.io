Who Is Marc M.?

I'm a simple fellow.

As an aspiring web-dev, I have created this site to introduce myself. I am an husband, a stay-at-home father, and a full-time student. Currently, I am homeschooling my eldest child while I pursue a career in the tech-industry. I'm a thoroughbred Californian. I was born and raised in Santa Cruz County. I grew up in Northern California. And have finally landed in the Central Valley. I live with my beautiful wife, and three children. My wife and I just recently celebrated the arrival of a baby girl we named Lucy. Being a stay-at-home dad is tough, but I wouldn't trade it in for the world. To tell you a little bit more about myself, I have created a few pages to take you through my childhood, on into adulthood. So...thanks for joining me!

  • Santa Cruz, California

    The Early 80's

    I was born in Santa Cruz County. Being near the coast I visited the beach often. One of my favorite amenities was the Santa Cruz Beach Boardwalk.

  • Northern California

    The Early 90's

    I moved to Northern California when I was ten years old. Northern California was different. I was away from my extended family, and didn't know many people. It was hard to make friends. One of my favorite past-times then was the movie theatres. I lived in up North until I was in my 20s.

  • The Bay Area

    A New Millenia

    I moved on down to San Mateo in the early 2000s. San Mateo is where I started taking my education seriously. I ventured into a Sociology major with the goal of helping others in some areas where I had struggled. I really enjoy volunteering my time to causes greater than myself. My time in the Bay Area was short, as an opportunity opened up down South.

  • The Central Valley

    Current

    From the Los Angeles area I moved to Fresno County where my new bride resided. She recognized that I had a passion for cooking, and so she encouraged me to attend Culinary school; but after much deliberation, I decided against that option. You will see nonetheless, that the desire to create something in the kitchen is still prevalent and growing each day. I have grown accustom to the Central Valley and it's smoldering heat. I have laid a foundation here, and would like to grow old here.
