Who Is Marc Murillo?

I'm a simple fellow.

As an aspiring web-dev, I have created this site to introduce myself.
I am an husband, a stay-at-home father, and a full-time student. Currently, I am homeschooling my eldest child while I pursue a career in the tech-industry. I'm a thoroughbred Californian. I was born and raised in Santa Cruz County. I grew up in Northern California. And have finally landed in the Central Valley.
I live with my beautiful wife, and three children. My wife and I just recently celebrated the arrival of a baby girl we named Lucy. Being a stay-at-home dad is tough, but I wouldn't trade it in for the world.
To tell you a little bit more about myself, I have created a few pages to take you through my childhood, on into adulthood. So...thanks for joining me!
